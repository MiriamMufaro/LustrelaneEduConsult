About Us

Lustrelane Education Consultancy

~Illuminating the Way for Learners~

At Lustrelane Education Consultancy, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a brighter future. Our mission is to illuminate the path for ambitious students from Zimbabwe, Zambia, Malawi, and Botswana, guiding them toward world-class higher education opportunities across the globe. With access to countries renowned for their strong academic reputation, affordability, and cultural richness, we ensure that students have the opportunity to pursue quality education that empowers them to achieve their dreams.

Who We Are

Lustrelane Education Consultancy is more than just an education placement service—we are mentors, advisors, and lifelong supporters. We recognize that the journey to studying abroad can feel complex and overwhelming, but with our expertise, we turn uncertainties into clear pathways. Like a guiding light, we help students and their families navigate every step of the process with confidence and clarity.

How We Illuminate the Path

Our consultancy provides a comprehensive suite of services designed to simplify the journey from application to arrival:

  • Academic Counseling

    • We help students discover their strengths and align them with suitable programs and universities in countries known for their academic excellence, affordability, and diverse cultural experiences.

  • University Placement Assistance

    • Our team ensures students apply to institutions that match their academic and career goals, maximizing their chances of admission.

  • Visa Guidance & Processing

    • We simplify visa applications by providing expert advice and assistance to ensure smooth approval.

  • Pre-Departure Orientation

    • Moving abroad is a major transition; we prepare students for cultural differences, academic expectations, and life in their chosen destination countries.

  • Post-Arrival Support

    • Our commitment doesn’t end once students arrive. We assist with accommodation, networking, and adaptation to ensure they settle comfortably.
